for women

Menstruation and sanitation during periods is important for several reasons. Firstly, access to safe and hygienic menstrual products and facilities can prevent the spread of infectious diseases and improve overall health outcomes for women and girls. Additionally, the ability to manage menstruation in a private and safe manner can help to reduce the social stigma and discrimination that can often be associated with periods. This can lead to increased self-esteem and confidence, especially for young girls, who may otherwise miss school or other opportunities because of their menstrual cycle. Furthermore, access to menstrual hygiene management can also have a positive impact on women's economic empowerment, as it can enable them to continue working or studying during their period without interruption. Overall, menstruation and sanitation during periods is an essential aspect of women's health and well-being, and should be treated as a priority.
